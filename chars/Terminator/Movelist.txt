Movement:

------------------------------------------------------------
A - Gun (up close - punch)
------------------------------------------------------------
B - Uzi (at close range while standing - kick);
------------------------------------------------------------
C - Plasmagun (up close - weapon hit)
------------------------------------------------------------
X - Shotgun;
------------------------------------------------------------
Y - Machine gun;
------------------------------------------------------------
Z - Rifle (possible shooting bursts when pressed again);
------------------------------------------------------------ 
Start - Tactical mode (not work in the air) - pressing the delay (homing mode against the enemy. For the purpose of switching to use the buttons to move forward and back. For a shot in tactical mode, release the button "Start")
------------------------------------------------------------

Specials:

Heavy Machine Gun - A+X or B+Y or C+Z 
------------------------------------------------------------
Mines - X+Y (not work in the air)
------------------------------------------------------------
Bazooka - Y+Z
------------------------------------------------------------
Plasmagrenades - B+C
------------------------------------------------------------
Grenade launcher - A+B
------------------------------------------------------------
